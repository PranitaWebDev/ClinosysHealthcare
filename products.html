At Clinosys, we offer a comprehensive suite of healthcare technology solutions under the Clinosys Hospital Information System (CHIS) umbrella, designed to optimize every facet of hospital management. Our integrated products include a robust Patient Management System handling all aspects of patient flow from OPD and IPD to admissions, discharge, and transfers, alongside sophisticated Billing & Financial Management capabilities supporting insurance, corporate, and cash billing with efficient TPA management. We also provide a cutting-edge Electronic Medical Records (EMR) system for digital prescriptions, comprehensive patient history, and seamless lab report integration, complemented by our Pharmacy & Inventory Management module for effective stock tracking and supplier integration, and our Laboratory & Radiology Management system enabling automated test management and reporting. Furthermore, CHIS features Operation Theater Management for streamlined surgery scheduling and anesthesia records, Doctor & Staff Management tools for efficient duty roster creation and payroll integration, and user-friendly Mobile Apps for both patients and doctors, facilitating convenient appointment booking and easy access to reports.
